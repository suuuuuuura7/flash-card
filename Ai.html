About Me

Hello, I'm Sarah Johnson

I'm a professional photographer with over 10 years of experience specializing in wedding, portrait, and commercial photography. My passion is capturing authentic moments and creating timeless images that tell your unique story.

Based in New York, I've had the privilege of working with clients across the United States and internationally. My work has been featured in several publications including Vogue, Wedding Magazine, and Professional Photographer.
